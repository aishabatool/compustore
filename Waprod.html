Support

· Warranty Policy

We at Online Computers Database Website are authorized to handle the warranty issues under the given limited authority from product distributors / manufacturers.

We provide warranty replacement once the warranty claim is approved by distributors / manufacturers. Although we try our best to give the warranty replacement at the earliest but this period may vary from 10 to 20 working days and under certain circumstances this may be extended to 30 days.

It is essential to understand certain rules relating to warranty issue.Some are summarized below :

  • Warranty means replacement with new product or repairs the same which ever is applicable.
  • The Warranty terms and conditions applied by manufacturers will also be considered on all warranty claims. The same may be viewed at the manufacturers websites.

        Intel - HP - Samsung - Kingston Seagate - Asus - Viewsonic

  • Warranty claims will only be entertained in case of manufacturing faults. Any fault developed due to man handling , borken parts, electricity surges/spikes, burnt elements on the warranty items, torn or tampered with Computers reserves the right to cancel such warranty claims.
  • The original proof of purchases must be presented at the time of warranty claim. Similarly the repair order (Issued at the time of receiving of warranty claim) is also essential to receive the replacement.
  • Warranty claim will not be entertained in case of software or Virus related defects/issues.
  • The Customer must read and accept the warranty terms at the time of purchase.
  • Despite our best efforts, please note that due to forced majore and any change in governing laws pertaining to import/export may result in hange of warranty terms and conditions.
